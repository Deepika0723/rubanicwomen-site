Return Policy

At Rubanic Women, we prioritize customer satisfaction. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

Return Eligibility

- Items must be returned within 14 days of delivery.
- Products must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging.
- Proof of purchase is required for all returns.

Non-Returnable Items

- Earrings (for hygiene reasons)
- Customized or personalized items
- Gift cards and sale items

Refund Process

- Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item and notify you.
- Approved returns will be refunded within 7 business days.
- Refunds will be processed to the original payment method.

How to Initiate a Return

- Email us at support@rubanicwomen.com with your order details.
- We will provide return instructions.
- Ship the item back to our return center.

If you have any questions, contact us at support@rubanicwomen.com
